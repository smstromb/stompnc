How to Make a Donation

No one should have to fight the battle with cancer alone. We hope you will join us in our efforts to provide a helping hand to those impacted by cancer when they need it most – during their fight with the disease.

STOMP the Monster™ NC is a North Carolina based not-for-profit corporation, 501(c)3. We rely on the generosity of private donors and our partners/sponsors in the business community to fund this important work. Donations may be made in memory or in honor of a loved one.
